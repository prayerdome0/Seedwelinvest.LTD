Company Registration (PACRA)

Register Your Business with Confidence Complete your company registration with the Patent and Companies Registration Agency (PACRA). We handle the entire registration process efficiently, ensuring your business is legally established and compliant with Zambian regulations.

ZRA VAT, TPIN & TOT Registration

Tax Registration Made Simple Complete your business registration with the Zambia Revenue Authority (ZRA). We assist with obtaining your TPIN (Taxpayer Identification Number) and registering for VAT (Value Added Tax) and TOT (Turnover Tax), helping your business meet all tax compliance requirements.

Workers compesation

Protect Your Business and Employees Register your company with the Workers' Compensation Fund Control Board.
